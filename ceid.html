The CEID was founded in 1979 and began its operation in 1980 and is essentially the leading part in the field of Computer Technology, Informatics and Communications in Greece. In a short time, developed into one of the best university departments in Greece with a large number of candidates every year. Involved in teaching and research in science and technology of computers and the study of their applications.
